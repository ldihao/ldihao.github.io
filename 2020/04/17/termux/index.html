问题

其他设备无法使用 ssh 连接到手机

  • Pick ports higher than 8000 (if still problems, try in range 30000-50000).
  • Ensure that isolation mode is disabled on your Wi-Fi router.
  • Ensure that no VPN is running on your Android device.
  • Ensure that you are connecting to correct IP.

is this problem of my android firewall ? or is it problem with termux

  • Definitely not with Termux. Possible causes: restrictions of your Wi-Fi router or device’s ROM or just using wrong IP.

结果是由于一直挂着 v2ray 导致其他设备连不到手机端口（可以 ping 通，但端口无法连接）。
